How We Craft It

Every Day1Craft lure begins with precision-engineered molds. From the first prototype to the final product, each piece meets high standards in durability, balance, and realism. Our team blends modern techniques with traditional craftsmanship—pouring, trimming, coating, and hand-painting. Each lure goes through quality checks before hitting the water.
