Top-Selling Lures

KIFM55 — Precision in Motion

The KIFM55 is engineered for technical anglers who demand exact lure control. With its 55mm profile and balanced internal weighting, it delivers pinpoint casting accuracy and mimics a wounded baitfish with lifelike twitch action. Built with corrosion-resistant hardware and a UV-reactive finish, the KIFM55 is your go-to when fish get selective.

KIM40 — Aggression Unleashed

Designed for rapid strikes, the KIM40 features an aggressive wobble and a compact 40mm body ideal for pressured waters. It dives quickly and maintains a tight action at high speeds, making it perfect for both fast retrieves and stop-and-go techniques. Reinforced with a hard coating and razor-sharp treble hooks, this lure turns cautious chasers into committed biters.

How We Craft It

Every Day1Craft lure begins with precision-engineered molds, carefully developed by experienced makers. From the first prototype to the final product, we ensure each piece meets our high standards in durability, balance, and realism.

Our team combines modern techniques with traditional craftsmanship — pouring, trimming, coating, and painting each lure by hand. Once shaped, each lure goes through quality checks before hitting the water. Because for us, it’s not just a product. It’s a tool built to perform, cast after cast.
